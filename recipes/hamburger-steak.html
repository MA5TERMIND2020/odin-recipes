Hamburger Steak with Mashed Potatoes and Gravy

So simple yet so delicious!

This recipe definitely makes my Top Five list of favorite foods. One of the things I love most about it is the creamy mashed potatoes paired with that seasoned, juicy hamburger steak. Its an awesome confort food that's bursting with flavor!

Ingredients

Hamburger Steaks

  • 1 lb of Hamburger Meat
  • 2 Tbsp of Montreal Steak Seasoning

Mashed Potatoes

  • 1 1/2 cups of Water
  • 1 1/3 cups of Instant Mashed Potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp of Margerine
  • 1/4 tsp of Salt
  • 1 Tbsp of Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 cup of Milk

Gravy

  • 1 Sliced Onion
  • 1 packet of brown gravy mix
  • 1 cup of Water

Steps

  1. Place a non-stick frying pan on the stove and turn burner to medium-low heat.
  2. Place a small pot on the stove, pour in 1 1/3 cups water, 2 Tbsp margerine, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1 Tbsp garlic powder. Turn this one to high heat.
  3. Combine the Montreal Steak seasoning to the hamburger meat, mixing it all together.
  4. Shape the hamburger patties to your liking and place them, along with the sliced onions, into the frying pan.
  5. Once the pot of water begins to boil, remove from heat, then stir in the instant potatoes, and finally the milk.
  6. Flip the hamburger steaks once they get done on the bottom, then remove them to the plate once they are fully cooked. Be sure to stir the onions in the meantime to make sure they cook evenly.
  7. Add the packet of brown gravy mix and cup of water to the frying pan. Bring the gravy to a boil while stirring frequently.
  8. Once the gravy mixture reaches the desired consistency,reduce heat to a simmer, place the mashed potatoes on the plate, top it all off with the gravy, and enjoy!
